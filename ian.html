Welcome to IMDB Actor Analysis Page

These page illustrates a films financial success using support vector machines based upon actors in a film and the actors world gross income

Overview


I wanted to build a model that would take in the actors name, as well as the total world gross icnocme for the actor. It would then predict the total gross income of a movie the actor appeared in.

I wanted to see if an actor does make a difference in the wolrd gross income of a film.

Support Vector Machines

The training data accuracy and the test data accuracy were both significantly low. The accuracy of my training data was -0.096, and my test data -0.099

Actor Affect on World Gross Income

Many of the actors appear to have little affect on the world gross income. But there are a few that do appear to have an affect, there are outliers as well. The ouliers may show a small group of actors that do have a major affect on world gross income.

R2 for my model

The R2 for my model was significantly low at -0.0989, showing the data was a poor fit to the model. But this was much higher than my linear regression model and the logistics model.

Conclusion/h5>

The data was a poor fit for all three models attempted. The Data may require more cleaning and transforming before training and testing.
